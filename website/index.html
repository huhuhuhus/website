Validator Responsibilities

As a validator, your responsibilities include:

  • Securing the network by validating transactions.
  • Participating in consensus protocols.
  • Running and maintaining validator nodes.
  • Voting on protocol upgrades and improvements.
  • Ensuring uptime and stability of the network.
